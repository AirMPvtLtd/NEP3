How to Use This Circuit Builder

  • Drag and drop components from the panel to the workspace
  • Connect components by dragging from one connection point to another
  • Click on components to change their properties (like resistance or voltage)
  • Click the switch to open/close the circuit
  • Press "Simulate Circuit" to see current flow and calculate circuit values
  • Press "Reset All" to clear the workspace and start over
  • Build a complete circuit with a battery and bulb to light it up!
