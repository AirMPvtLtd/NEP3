Description of Service

SPYRAL NEP Workbench is an AI-powered adaptive learning platform aligned with India's National Education Policy (NEP 2020). The Platform provides:

  • Personalised learning tools for students (Grades 1–12), including simulations, AI challenges, practice zones, and competency tracking.
  • Teacher tools including lesson notes builder, question generator, and simulator builder.
  • Institutional analytics and Student Performance Index (SPI) reporting for school administrators.
  • Parent dashboards for progress visibility.
  • SPYRAL Architecture — a keyword-first AI governance framework for institutions integrating AI.

NEP 2020 Alignment: All educational content and assessments are mapped to NEP 2020 competency frameworks and NCERT guidelines. We do not replace curriculum — we enhance implementation.

Student Data Protection

Student data is treated with the highest level of protection. We comply with India's Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023 and applicable state-level regulations.

For students under the age of 18, the school institution acts as the data fiduciary on behalf of parents/guardians. By enrolling students on the Platform, the school confirms it has obtained appropriate parental/guardian consent.

Data Minimisation

Student academic data is collected only to the extent necessary for generating learning insights and SPI reports. We do not build commercial profiles of students.

Access Controls

  • Student data is accessible only to the student themselves, their assigned teachers, parents linked to their account, and the school administrator.

Data Portability

Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary:

  • Active accounts: Data is retained for the duration of the school's subscription plus 12 months thereafter.

  • Deletion requests: Processed within 30 days of a verified request from the school admin, parent, or adult user.
  • Backups: Encrypted backups are purged within 90 days of the data deletion request.
