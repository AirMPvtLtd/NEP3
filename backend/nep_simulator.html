NEP Learning Outcomes:

  • Conceptual understanding of force and its effects on motion.
  • Real-world application of force in everyday scenarios.
  • Critical thinking to analyze the relationship between force, mass, and acceleration.
  • Observation and inference skills to predict the outcome of applying different forces.

Real-world / industry use-case:

This simulator can be used to understand the principles of force in various engineering applications such as designing bridges, understanding vehicle dynamics, and predicting the motion of objects in space.

What student learns by interaction:

  • How force affects the motion of an object.
  • The relationship between force, mass, and acceleration.
  • The concept of Newton's Second Law of Motion.
